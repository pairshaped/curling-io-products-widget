To include this widget

For your leagues, just add the following snippet to your page(s), replacing "club-domain" with the domain we've assigned to you, and "leagues" with the section you want to display, from "leagues", "competitions", or "products".

      <div id="curlingio-registration-widget"></div>
      <script>
        var app = Elm.Main.init({
          node: document.getElementById("curlingio-registration-widget"),
          flags: { host: "https://club-domain.curling.io", section: "leagues" }
        })
      </script>
